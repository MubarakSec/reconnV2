SimpliSafe's mission is to make every home secure. Delivering on that means putting our customers at the center of everything we do to improve their safety and comfort. Our home security solutions are designed with cutting-edge technology yet are affordable to purchase, easy to install and don’t require contracts. We protect more than four million people throughout the United States and the United Kingdom against everything from intruders to fires, water damage and more.

As part of our commitment to our customers, we take data privacy and security issues very seriously. By engaging with talented security researchers across the globe through programs like Bugcrowd, SimpliSafe can more effectively identify and resolve suspected vulnerabilities.

Thank you for participating in our program and helping protect SimpliSafe and its customers from potential threats.
Now Public in September 2025!

We are excited to have this program open to the public. In these first weeks, we expect increased activity, so please keep these in mind while testing our targets:

    Use an account tied with your @bugcrowdninja.com email for all testing. We will block and restrict suspicious activity from other accounts. Submissions that involve other email addresses will be marked as Out of Scope.
    The auth.simplisafe.com subdomain handles authentication and is owned by Auth0. Since SimpliSafe doesn’t own the system, it is Out of Scope for testing and suspicious activity will be blocked. Normal behavior with this system is expected as part of testing.
    Be a good neighbor while testing our systems and refrain from activities that could affect system availability. We're co-workers now, so let's be kind to each other.

If you have any questions about our systems or want to know more information to help test, feel free to reach out to support and they can pass the questions along.

Thanks for joining us in our bug bounty program!
Ratings/Rewards:

For the initial prioritization/rating of findings, this program will use the Bugcrowd Vulnerability Rating Taxonomy. However, it is important to note that in some cases a vulnerability priority will be modified due to its likelihood or impact. In any instance where an issue is downgraded, a full, detailed explanation will be provided to the researcher - along with the opportunity to appeal, and make a case for a higher priority.

Good luck, and happy hunting!

Testing is only authorized on the targets listed as in scope. Any domain/property of SimpliSafe not listed in the targets section is out of scope. This includes any/all subdomains not listed above. If you happen to identify a security vulnerability on a target that is not in scope, but it demonstrably belongs to SimpliSafe, you can report it to this program. However, be aware that it is ineligible for rewards or points-based compensation.
Access:

All of the targets are accessible via the public internet and available. Simplisafe is designed for US and UK based customers. Access from other countries may inhibit testing.

Our systems help keep millions of our customers safe. Testing and research on a system can impact its ability to do so. Do not perform any testing on a system that you or others are actively using.

If you encounter a We are unable to create your account at this time error during account creation, reach out to Bugcrowd support or add a comment in a submission with your IP address and the email you used.
Credentials:

To gain access to the application, please sign up for an account using your @bugcrowdninja.com email address. For more info regarding @bugcrowdninja email addresses, see here. With that email address, you can perform a Forgot Password reset to gain access to the account. You may register for accounts here.

PLEASE NOTE: Submissions that are created without using your @bugcrowdninja email will be marked as Out of Scope.
Exclusions:

We do not allow and will not review submissions based on:

    brute force authentication requests
    denial of service
    web crawling bots
    social engineering
    physical attacks

    minor website misconfigurations such as 404 codes and others

    Any vulnerabilities found in our vendors’ products, e.g., third party software libraries, fall outside this policy’s scope and should be reported directly to the vendor according to their vulnerability disclosure policy.

    Obviously, if we are using a vulnerable library or component and are affected by the vulnerability, that would be a finding we want to be submitted. But just utilizing a library that has a vulnerability shouldn't be a qualified finding.

N-Day Policy:

    When N-Day bugs are released to the public, we will consider these as in scope after 14 days has gone by
        e.g: N-day released on 01/01/2023, we would consider it in-scope on 01/15/2023

Out of Scope

Websites on subdomains linked to from simplisafe.com are out of scope. These include but are not limited to:

    careers.simplisafe.com
    clicks.simplisafe.com
    library.simplisafe.com
    livechat.simplisafe.com
    press.simplisafe.com
    support.simplisafe.com

Safe Harbor:

When conducting vulnerability research according to this policy, we consider this research to be:

    Authorized in accordance with the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) (and/or similar state laws), and we will not initiate or support legal action against you for accidental, good faith violations of this policy;
    Exempt from the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), and we will not bring a claim against you for circumvention of technology controls;
    Exempt from restrictions in our Terms & Conditions that would interfere with conducting security research, and we waive those restrictions on a limited basis for work done under this policy; and
    Lawful, helpful to the overall security of the Internet, and conducted in good faith.
    You are expected, as always, to comply with all applicable laws.

If at any time you have concerns or are uncertain whether your security research is consistent with this policy, please create a ticket with Bugcrowd Support for clarification before proceeding.
